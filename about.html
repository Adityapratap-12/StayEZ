About StayEZ

Discover who we are and what drives us to make your stay unforgettable.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, StayEZ originated from a simple idea: to provide seamless, reliable vacation rental experiences for travellers in India. Our founders identified the need for a trustworthy platform that connects renters with beautiful and affordable properties, supported by excellent customer service.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to making every stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. Our mission is to bridge the gap between travelers and property owners by offering an intuitive, easy-to-use platform that ensures quality, safety, and happiness for all parties involved.
